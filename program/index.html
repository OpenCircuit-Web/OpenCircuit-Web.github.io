9:00am Welcome Talk from Organizers
Lau Ka Kei from HKUST and FreeCircuit TECH
Ka Kei will talk about the motivation of organizing this event and share about the overview of full day's schedule. He will also share some insights and experience on the developemt of Open-sourced hardware-Software Co-design from industry-side and Academy-side.
9:30am Invited Talk from Insistitue of Physics, UCAS
Yiwen Ma (UCAS)
Yiwen will briefly introduce their recent work on crynogenic computing platform based on TaSeIn material and he will demonstrate how they benefit from the open-source projects to better solve their problems in condensed physics research. He will also talk about their on-going work on Single-Flux-Quantum(SFQ) Computing Accelerator.
