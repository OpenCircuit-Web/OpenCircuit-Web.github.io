Possible Topics

  • Open-source EDA Tools and Frameworks for Electronic Circuit Design: Exploring the latest in electronic design automation tools and frameworks for circuit design.
  • Open-source FPGA/CGRA Projects for Edge and Cloud AI Applications: Innovative FPGA and CGRA projects enhancing AI at the edge and in the cloud.
  • Reconfigurable Superconducting or Quantum Computing Accelerators: Advancements in accelerators for superconducting and quantum computing.
  • Reconfigurable Photonic Computing Processor or Accelerator Design for Various Applications: Design of reconfigurable photonic computing processors and accelerators.
  • Open-source EDA Tools and Frameworks for Photonic Accelerator Design: EDA tools and frameworks for photonic accelerator design.

Submission Tracks and Guidelines

Poster and In-progress Paper

Length: 2 pages, excluding references. Format: ACM conference template.

  • Preliminary or in-progress research showcasing novel ideas or methodologies.
  • Experience reports on the challenges and successes of open-source accelerators.

Special Issue and Invited Paper

Length: 6-8 pages, excluding references. Format: ACM conference template.

  • Detailed experience reports on deploying open-source accelerators.
  • Thought-provoking essays and surveys on reconfigurable computing practices.
  • Reflective retrospectives on influential projects, tools, and techniques.
  • Updates on recent progress and innovative solutions in the field.
